Pasta Alfredo

Back to Odin Recipes

Description

Pasta Alfredo is a rich and creamy Italian-American dish made with fettuccine noodles tossed in a smooth sauce of butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingridients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine in salted boiling water; drain.
  2. Melt butter in a pan, add garlic (optional).
  3. Add cream, simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Stir in Parmesan, mix until smooth.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Toss pasta in sauce until fully coated.
  7. Serve hot, garnish with parsley if desired.
